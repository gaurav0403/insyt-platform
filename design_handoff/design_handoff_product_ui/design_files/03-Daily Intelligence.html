The hallmarking question reaches Kerala.

A 14-minute Malayalam YouTube segment, posted overnight, has become the day's largest source of negative discussion — without yet appearing in English press.

The segment, by independent journalist Reshma Pillai, examines BIS hallmarking compliance among regional jewellery chains and names Kalyan twice in a 90-second passage¹. By 05:00 IST the upload had crossed 412,000 views and 1,840 comments, the majority of them adversarial in sentiment.

The story has not yet been picked up by Manorama Online or Mathrubhumi, but the gravity of those sources, combined with the segment's penetration through Kerala's WhatsApp networks², suggests it will surface in print by tomorrow's edition.

The trajectory closely matches the 2023 Titan hallmarking moment, which moved from a single regional source into national press over 38 hours and required a CEO-level response. We expect a similar window here.
