What actually happened

The Tanishq comms team became aware of the Pillai segment at hour 31, when the first English-press inquiry arrived from Mint. A statement was drafted, reviewed, and issued at hour 41 — by which point the story had reached the front page of Mint's print edition and BSE had flagged unusual options activity.
