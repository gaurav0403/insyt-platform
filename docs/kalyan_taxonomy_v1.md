## 13. Risk & Crisis Categories

### Crisis typology (for narrative classification)
| Category | Example triggers |
|---|---|
| **Product quality** | Adulteration claims, purity disputes, hallmark issues |
| **Customer service** | Store-level disputes, billing complaints going viral |
| **Employee/internal** | Glassdoor clusters, union issues, safety incidents |
| **Ambassador-linked** | Ambassador controversies splashing onto brand |
| **Financial rumour** | Tax raid rumours, governance, auditor issues (Jan 2025 precedent) |
| **Regulatory** | SEBI/BIS/GST actions or investigations |
| **Family/promoter** | Succession, pledging, personal legal issues |
| **Competitive attack** | Orchestrated narrative campaigns by rivals |
| **Cyber/fraud** | Fake websites, impersonation, scam schemes using brand name |
| **Geopolitical** | Gold import policy, Middle East-specific issues |
| **Social/cultural** | Advertising controversy, cultural insensitivity |

### Severity scoring dimensions
- Velocity (how fast is it spreading)
- Reach (how many unique accounts engaged)
- Amplifier quality (verified journalists vs. anonymous accounts)
- Regional concentration
- Cross-platform spread
- Media pickup indicator (has mainstream press covered it yet)
- Regulatory-adjacency (could it trigger filing obligation)

### Known historical crisis patterns (for model training)
- **Jan 2025 stock crash** — social media allegations of IT raids, bribery rumours → 37% stock decline over 2 weeks before corporate clarification. *Perfect case study for dossier.*
- **Dubai adulteration claims (2018)** — criminal charges against 5 accused eventually filed by Dubai Police
- **Advertising controversies** — past ads withdrawn after criticism
